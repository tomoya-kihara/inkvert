# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview

Inkvert ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide
Inkvert processes photos that you select from your photo library. These photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

### Automatically collected information
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data to serve relevant ads:

- Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA)
- IP address
- Coarse location data
- App usage data

For more information, please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

---

## How We Use Information

- Photos you select are processed locally on your device only
- We do not store, transmit, or share your photos
- Ad-related data is collected by Google AdMob solely for advertising purposes

---

## In-App Purchases

Inkvert offers a one-time purchase to remove ads ("Remove Ads"). Purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

---

## Data Retention

Inkvert does not retain any personal data. All photo processing happens on-device and nothing is stored on our servers.

---

## Children's Privacy

Inkvert is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

---
